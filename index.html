In 2001 we opened New York City's first quality-driven coffee shop. Our goals were simple: to serve great coffee to our friends and neighbors in the East Village. Seventeen years and five shops later, our goals are the same. We are focused on remaining genuine, consistent and serving great coffee without the pretense.
